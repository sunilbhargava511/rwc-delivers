## About
Family-run restaurant specializing in authentic Turkish cuisine. Chef Hasan brings over 30 years of experience in food and hospitality. Everything is prepared from scratch, including pita bread cooked to order. Strong focus on traditional dishes including kebabs, pides (Turkish flatbread boats), mezze spreads, and classic Turkish desserts.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Overwhelmingly positive. Food described as outstanding, authentic, fresh, and flavorful. Everything made from scratch. Dishes described as "artful" and "memorable."

**Service:** Chef Hasan personally engages with diners, explains the menu, and makes recommendations. Staff is warm and helpful.

**Atmosphere:** Warm, casual, welcoming with an authentic Turkish feel. The chef's interaction with guests adds to the experience.

**Popular Dishes:** Beef kebabs, lamb kebab, Nomadic Pide, kunefe (dessert), mezze combinations, hummus, tzatziki ("best anywhere"), babaganoush, fresh bread, lahmacun.

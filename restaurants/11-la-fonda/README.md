## About
The brick-and-mortar sibling of the popular Los Carnalitos food truck. Latinx-owned, women-led restaurant celebrating the rich culinary heritage of Mexico City. Uses traditional techniques including hand-pressed tortillas and complex, slow-simmered sauces. Features a premium tequila bar.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Highly praised for authenticity and depth of flavor. Handmade tortillas pressed fresh throughout the day are a standout. Mole poblano described as "velvety and complex."

**Service:** Distinguished by an all-women front-of-house team. "Super friendly," attentive, and knowledgeable.

**Atmosphere:** Vibrantly decorated with rainbow-colored chairs, brick-red walls, rustic folk art. "Joyfully festive."

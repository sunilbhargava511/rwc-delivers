## About
Chef Momo Farouq, originally from Morocco, immigrated to Italy at age 16 and ran his pizzeria in Lake Como, Italy for 35 years. He relocated to downtown Redwood City after meeting his wife Sarika Batra. Features seasonally changing menu of organic, scratch-made sourdough crust pizzas, handmade pastas, antipasti, and desserts. Blends Italian tradition with Moroccan-inspired creations.

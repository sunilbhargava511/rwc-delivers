## About
Chef/Owner Tim Fabatz trained with Michel Guerard in Southern France and Francesco Berardinelli in Tuscany. Mediterranean-influenced New American cuisine with French, Italian, Spanish, and Asian touches. Emphasizes freshest fish, shellfish, meat, and local sustainable produce. Features in-house oak-fired pizza. Waterfront location on a lagoon in Redwood Shores with year-round heated patio and outdoor fireplace. Operating for 25 years.

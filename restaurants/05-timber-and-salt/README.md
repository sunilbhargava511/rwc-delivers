## About
Since 2015, Timber & Salt has been serving dressed-up comfort food featuring quality steaks, seafood, craft cocktails, and fresh seasonal dishes. The bar program showcases housemade syrups, bitters, and tinctures. Also offers unique whiskey tastings.

## About
Established in 2012, Vesta specializes in handcrafted wood-fired pizzas and dynamic small plates. They offer quirky Neapolitan pizzas with creative California-influenced toppings, alongside shareable appetizers, salads, and desserts. Featured in San Francisco Magazine.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Consistently praised as "absolutely delicious." Unique flavor combinations and fresh, high-quality ingredients. Wood-fired pizzas compared favorably to top SF California-Italian spots.

**Service:** Friendly, attentive, efficient, and knowledgeable about the menu.

**Atmosphere:** Warm, intimate, and lively. Popular for date nights. Can get busy and noisy during peak hours. Wood-fired oven is a visual centerpiece.

## About
Established in 2009 by Chef Donato Scotti. Reflects a culinary philosophy of using fresh, simple ingredients in inventive ways, showcasing each region of Italy. Reopened late 2025 with new executive chef Marco Bertoldo and an almost entirely reimagined menu of smaller, sharable dishes. Menu changes weekly based on seasonal availability.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Highly praised. "Truly authentic Italian" with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Uses farmer's market produce and imported Italian artisanal products (fresh burrata, prosciutto, grappa, olive oil).

**Service:** Generally "outstanding" and "excellent." Staff described as kind and efficient.

**Atmosphere:** Warm, casually elegant. Contemporary decor with open kitchen. Three dining rooms plus patio. Popular for date night.

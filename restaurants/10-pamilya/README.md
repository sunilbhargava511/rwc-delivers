## About
"Pamilya" means "family" in Tagalog. Founded by Jhed Yuzon, whose father owns PatioFilipino in Millbrae and maternal grandmother founded the Goldilocks Bakeshop chain. Started as a ghost kitchen (CloudKitchen) in 2022, opened brick-and-mortar in mid-2024. Proudly serves authentic Filipino comfort food made with heart, heritage, and home-style flavors. Fast-casual, bowl-based ordering with customizable options.

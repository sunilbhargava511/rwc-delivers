## About
Named after La Viga, Mexico City's massive seafood market. The restaurant brings coastal Mexican flavors to Redwood City, blending traditional marisqueria cooking with modern California touches. Menu emphasizes fresh seafood, seasonal ingredients, and sustainability, alongside a curated bar program featuring mezcals, tequilas, and seasonal cocktails.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Reviewers consistently praise the freshness of the seafood. Ceviches are "always incredibly fresh," nothing is ever greasy, and seasonal ingredients shine. The kitchen highlights artisan craftsmanship by Chef Jorge.

**Service:** Described as "enthusiastic service" — staff are warm and attentive. The bartender is frequently called out as "super nice." Community-style hospitality is a recurring theme.

**Atmosphere:** Colorful murals and rustic decor evoke the charm of a seaside Mexican village. Open kitchen lets diners watch food being prepared. Lunch is "nice and quiet," while dinner draws a lively crowd.

**Popular Dishes:** Tallarines con Mariscos (signature), fish tacos (fried snapper), Camarones a la Diabla, empanadas de mariscos, made-to-order ceviches, margaritas, Palomas.

## About
Casual-elegant Indian dining featuring regional flavors "from Delhi to California." Menu spans Delhi street food to Mughlai classics, crafted with authentic recipes blended with fresh California ingredients. Women-owned by Sunny and Swati. All meats are Halal. Open since October 2013.

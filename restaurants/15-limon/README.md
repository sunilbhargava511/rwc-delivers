## About
Part of a beloved Bay Area restaurant chain, a cornerstone of Peruvian cuisine for more than two decades. Known for vibrant Peruvian flavors, modern dining experience, and dynamic atmosphere. Menu features ceviches, rotisserie chicken, wok-fired saltados, and seafood, with influences from traditional Peruvian and Chifa (Peruvian-Chinese) cooking.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Ceviche is the standout — "best ceviche in the Bay Area." Beautiful presentation. Strong cocktails (Pisco Sours).

**Service:** Mostly praised as responsive and friendly, though occasionally inconsistent.

**Atmosphere:** Vibrant, "full, loud, and fun." Popular for special occasions. Lofted dining room.

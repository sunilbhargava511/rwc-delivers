## About
Authentic dishes from the Levant and Mediterranean (Jordan, Lebanon, Syria, Greece, Turkey), prepared over 100% mesquite wood fire. Served tapas-style — simple, vibrant, and meant to be shared. Founded by brothers Jordan and Saif Makableh, who converted their family's struggling San Bruno market into a thriving restaurant concept.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Wood-grilled meats are the star. The Double Kebab Plate, 6-Hour Lamb Shank, and Crispy Cauliflower appetizer receive the most praise. Street-Style Wraps called "to die for." Strong vegetarian options.

**Service:** Ordering is via QR code at the table. Praised for efficiency even when busy, though some find the system impersonal.

**Atmosphere:** Lively, beautiful ambiance with spacious interior. Pet-friendly patio with heaters. Expect long waits on weekends (up to an hour).

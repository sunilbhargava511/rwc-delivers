## About
Waterfront dining experience centered around a custom live-fire hearth at Westpoint Harbor marina. Food is smoked, grilled, or cooked in ember. Seasonally evolving, seafood-forward menu. Founded by MeeSun Boice and Chef Parke Ulrich (also of Waterbar, Epic Steakhouse). Opened January 2024. Features a 1,200-gallon jellyfish tank with 150+ moon jellyfish. 500 ft of guest dock space — arrive by boat or kayak.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Dishes described as intentional, fresh, and flavorful. Oysters, ahi tuna tartare, wagyu, and whole-roasted dorade are exceptional. Warm milk bread is "legendary."

**Service:** Many praise attentive staff. Some note uneven pacing during busy periods.

**Atmosphere:** Universally praised. Jellyfish tank is "jaw-dropping." Marina views, open kitchen with live-fire hearth, teak-inspired decor.

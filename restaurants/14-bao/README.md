## About
BAO follows the Cantonese tradition of "one tea and two bites" — serving delicate, sweet, and savory treats meant to delight. Full menu of dim sum favorites plus traditional entrees served as small plates ideal for sharing. Every item made fresh to order with handmade Chinese cuisine techniques.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** Items made fresh to order; handmade quality of dim sum praised. Shanghai Xiao Long Bao and Baked BBQ Pork Bao are standouts.

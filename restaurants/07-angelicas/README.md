## About
Family-owned and operated since 2006 by Executive Chef Angelica. Located in the historic district of downtown Redwood City. Features vibrant California cuisine with a Latin touch — savory mole, succulent steaks, fresh oysters, and Peruvian ceviche. Also offers brunch and live entertainment.

## Yelp Review Summary

**Food Quality:** "Flavorful and cooked to perfection." Seafood (oysters, ceviche, octopus) gets particular acclaim. Steaks noted as succulent.

**Service:** "Spot on, efficient, friendly and present." Quick, attentive, accommodating for large parties.

**Atmosphere:** Elegant yet warm. Lush patio with floral arrangements. Live piano music on weekends. Beautiful garden/courtyard.
